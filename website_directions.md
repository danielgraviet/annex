Create a polished landing page for a startup called **Annex**.

## Concept

Annex is a curated network of extraordinary industrial and commercial workspaces for ambitious teams.

The business helps startups and SMBs find flexible workspace inside underutilized spaces like aircraft hangars, warehouses, fabrication shops, industrial campuses, and creative production facilities.

The goal of this website is not to build a full marketplace yet. The goal is to test interest, create excitement, and collect waitlist signups from founders, startups, SMBs, and potential space hosts.

## Positioning

Annex is not traditional coworking.
Annex is not generic commercial real estate.
Annex is not beige office space.

Annex is for builders who want to work somewhere with energy, character, and operational usefulness.

Core tagline:

**Extraordinary workspaces for ambitious teams.**

Supporting message:

**Find flexible space inside hangars, warehouses, workshops, and industrial campuses built for companies doing real work.**

## Design Direction

The site should feel:
- cinematic
- industrial
- modern
- premium
- founder-focused
- slightly rebellious
- practical, not gimmicky

Avoid:
- generic coworking visuals
- corporate real estate clichés
- smiling office stock photos
- bright SaaS gradients
- overly playful design

Visual inspiration:
- aircraft hangars
- warehouses
- concrete
- steel
- workshop lighting
- startup teams building physical things
- dark backgrounds with strong contrast
- spacious layouts
- editorial typography

## Recommended Tech Stack

Use:
- Next.js
- TypeScript
- Tailwind CSS
- shadcn/ui if useful
- Framer Motion for subtle animations if already available

## Site Structure

Build a single-page landing page with these sections:

---

## 1. Hero Section

Headline:

**Extraordinary workspaces for ambitious teams.**

Subheadline:

**Annex helps startups and growing businesses find flexible space inside hangars, warehouses, workshops, and industrial campuses — places with room to build, test, create, and grow.**

Primary CTA:

**Join the waitlist**

Secondary CTA:

**List your space**

Hero visual:
Create a cinematic mock visual/card showing an industrial workspace listing, such as:

**Aircraft Hangar Annex**
Austin, TX  
8,000 sq ft available  
Ideal for aerospace, robotics, drones, and engineering teams

Include visual cues like:
- available space
- flexible terms
- industrial power
- high ceilings
- shared operational environment

---

## 2. Problem Section

Title:

**Great companies should not have to start in beige offices.**

Body copy:

Too many ambitious teams end up in generic offices, expensive leases, or spaces that do not fit the way they actually work. At the same time, industrial and commercial tenants often have extra space sitting unused.

Annex connects these two realities.

---

## 3. Featured Spaces Section

Title:

**Work from places with character.**

Create 4 example listing cards:

### Aircraft Hangar Annex
Austin, TX  
For aerospace, drones, robotics, and engineering teams.

### Robotics Warehouse
San Francisco Bay Area  
Flexible industrial space for hardware and automation startups.

### Fabrication Studio
Los Angeles, CA  
Workshop-style space for makers, designers, and production teams.

### Builder Compound
Denver, CO  
Shared industrial campus for technical teams that need room to operate.

Each card should include:
- location
- ideal tenant type
- approximate square footage
- 2–3 feature tags
- “Request access” button

These can be mock listings. Make that feel natural, not fake or unfinished.

---

## 4. Why Annex Section

Title:

**The environment is part of the product.**

Copy:

The right space can help a team recruit better, build faster, impress customers, and feel like they are part of something bigger. Annex is designed for companies that want more than desks and drywall.

Include 3 feature blocks:

### Built for builders
Spaces for teams working on hardware, robotics, aerospace, design, production, logistics, and applied technology.

### Flexible by design
Access portions of larger spaces without committing to an entire long-term commercial lease.

### More than square footage
Find environments with energy, utility, and a story worth telling.

---

## 5. For Hosts Section

Title:

**Have unused space? Turn it into momentum.**

Copy:

If your company has extra industrial or commercial space, Annex helps you connect with ambitious teams that can bring energy, revenue, and community into your environment.

CTA:

**List your space**

Include host benefits:
- offset lease costs
- activate unused space
- bring in complementary companies
- support local builders

---

## 6. Waitlist Section

Title:

**Find your next workspace before everyone else does.**

Copy:

Annex is starting with a curated set of unique industrial and commercial spaces. Join the waitlist to get early access.

Form fields:
- name
- email
- company
- location
- what kind of space are you looking for?

CTA button:

**Join the waitlist**

Also include a secondary host form/CTA:

**Have space to share? Tell us about it.**

---

## 7. Footer

Include:
